Instructions

How to play :

1. Select the difficulty you wish to play.
2. Press start game to begin.
3. Click on an empty square and then select a number from the keypad.
4. You can only use each number once in any row, column and square.
5. Avoid guessing answers instead use a process of elimination.
6. Sit back, relax and give your brain a soothing workout.
